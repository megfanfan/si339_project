Research

Pediatric Health Communication

University of Michigan School of Information

I help my mentor design and conduct scenario-based interviews, design workshops, and discussion activities for kids with chronic illnesses and their parents. We then use the data gathered from these interactions to develop a high fidelity prototype that improves patient to caregiver communication within pediatric healthcare.

Speak to Your Health Community Survey

University of Michigan Institute for Social Research

I focused on user-centered design principles to build attractive and intuitive interfaces for the Speak to Your Health Community Survey. I also interviewed community partners and summarized community-based participatory research findings through online articles.
